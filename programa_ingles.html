Zona Leste - Autolabs
The first two days of the event aims to dissect Autolabs and the issues, experiences, difficulties and results of the tactical media labs at the east zone, also looking to make strategies for the development of future media labs.

Centro - FindEtático
The festivity at the east zone is going to be complemented by talks, workshops and interventions at the city center, where some of Autolabs workshop instructors plus guests will talk, show and produce works related to independent media, net.art, online collaboration and media activism, from a common online environment http://www.furtherstudio.org/live aiming to give a broad panorama of old and new independent media production, in a kind of game, using arguments such as images, sounds and animations. About the software. A media lab will also be built on sunday by a practical workshop on building labs with talks about methodologies, free software, authorship, autonomy etc. Co-laborate!
