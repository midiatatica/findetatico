Tactical Week:end
from June 24th to 27th

This weekend ('findE') is going to reflect upon Autolabs, tactical media labs in free software that were realized during six months in three districts of Sao Paulo's east zone: ermelino matarazzo, itaquera and são miguel paulista. The experience talks about new forms of media production with independent groups, inserted in a public politics context. The weekend also aims to give a broad panorama of similar projects from Holland (N5M/Proxyvision), Italy (Telestreet), France (Bureau D'Etudes) and England (Furtherfield).

Works produced during Autolabs are also going to be presented alongside free software media production workshops. Divided between presentation of works, a tour around the labs, debates and workshops-interventions at the city centre, the workshops instructors and guests will show some of the works produced at the labs, and will talk about their experiences on independent media production, radio and web streaming, net.art, forms and tools for online collaboration, free software, besides media activism and social-cultural mobilization.

On the 24th and 25th of june the activities will happen at Itaquera with bands, presentation of works produced in Autolabs, workshops on video, activist media and posters plus a web radio broadcasting the whole event. (link to be published here on the day). On the 26th and 27th the focus is the city center, gathering national and international guests such as David Garcia and Brian Holmes in an online jam with guests and public present at the venue to discuss presentially and through this audio-visual web environment the issues mentioned above. This virtual interface will also accommodate translocal interactions and will be fed by an navigator that will present before each section the context of the projects and related links.

Saturday 10 a.m. is dedicated to themes such TV, Radio and Web Streaming; 12 p.m. Strategies for Tactical Media, Graphic Arts and Independent Publishing; and 16 p.m. Global Collaborative Networks. On Sunday the 4-day week:end ends with an open workshop on Building Media Production Labs with Free Software, from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m. that will be contextualized with informal converging talks such as the culture of the open source code, softwares, methodologies and so on. You are invited to participate with us on this event by sending your images (.jpg), animations (.swf) and audios (.mp3) to the following link >

The weekend wants to make visible the tactical dialog between centre and periphery that was concretized during Autolabs and the non-territorialized, collaborative and independent logic of a networked media production. Let's co-laborate !
